Order Fulfillment (Delivery Driver Tasks)

Stakeholders

Description This use case covers the tasks involved in receiving, transporting, and delivering store orders to the designated locations.
Pre Condition(s) The store order has been fulfilled and is ready for pickup.
The delivery driver is assigned to the task.
Triggers Delivery driver receives a notification of an order to be delivered.

Basic Flow (Typical Course of Events)

Actor Action System/Result, Info required
1. Delivery Driver receives order details. System sends a notification with store order details (pickup time, items, destination).
2. Delivery Driver arrives at the warehouse. System verifies the delivery driver identity and order details.
3. Delivery Driver picks up the order. System confirms the order pickup and updates the status to "In Transit."
4. Delivery Driver transports the order to the store. System tracks the order's location during transportation.
5. Delivery Driver arrives at the store. System confirms delivery at destination and updates the order status to "Delivered."

Alternate Flow

Actor Action System/Result, Info required
1. Delivery Driver cannot reach store due to roadblock. System updates delivery status to "Delayed" and sends notification to both warehouse and store manager.
Conclusion Store order is successfully delivered to the store.
Post Condition(s) Order is marked as "Delivered" and inventory levels are updated at the store.

Additional Information

Business Rules
  • Delivery Drivers must confirm the order and their delivery before leaving the Warehouse.
  • Delivery must occur withing the defined time frame.
Special Requirements GPS integration to track delivery progress.
Constraints and Specifications Delivery must be completed by a specific time.
Technology and Data Integration with delivery tracking systems.
Frequency As needed based on store orders.
Assumptions All neccesary delivery documentation and inventory are availabile at the time of pickup.
