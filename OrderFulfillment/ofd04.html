Order Fulfillment (Report: Shipping Receipt)

Use Case ID OFD-04 Version 1.0
Use Case Name Report: Shipping Receipt Date 2024-11-02
Author Tony Power Complexity Medium
Priority Medium
Use Case Type:
☒ Business Requirement
☐ System Analysis
Source Business Requirement
Primary Actor Warehouse Worker
Other Actor(s) Delivery Driver, Acadia
Stakeholders
  • Warehouse Operations
  • Store Managers
Description This use case involves viewing or generating a shipping receipt report to confirm the details of items shipped, including pallet and vehicle information.
Pre Condition(s) Shipments have been processed, and details are recorded in the system.
Triggers Warehouse worker or delivery driver requests a shipping receipt.

Basic Flow (Typical Course of Events)

Actor Action System/Result, Info required
1. Warehouse Worker selects "Generate Shipping Receipt" option. System displays a form to enter relevant shipping details.
2. Warehouse Worker enters required details (e.g, shipment ID, destination) and submits the form. System retrieves shipment data and prepares the shipping receipt.
3. Warehouse Worker confirms report generation System generates the report, displaying details by pallet and vehicle size.
4. Warehouse Worker views or saves the generated report. System allows saving or printing of the report.

Alternate Flow

Actor Action System/Result, Info required
1. Delivery Driver cannot reach store due to roadblock. System updates delivery status to "Delayed" and sends notification to both warehouse and store manager.
Conclusion Store order is successfully delivered to the store.
Post Condition(s) Order is marked as "Delivered" and inventory levels are updated at the store.

Additional Information

Business Rules
  • Delivery Drivers must confirm the order and their delivery before leaving the Warehouse.
  • Delivery must occur withing the defined time frame.
Special Requirements GPS integration to track delivery progress.
Constraints and Specifications Delivery must be completed by a specific time.
Technology and Data Integration with delivery tracking systems.
Frequency As needed based on store orders.
Assumptions All neccesary delivery documentation and inventory are availabile at the time of pickup.
Notes / Open Issues None
